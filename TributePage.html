Xander

The Crown Prince of Nohr

As the eldest son of King Garon and wielder of the legendary blade Siegfried, Xander is the future king of Nohr.

Here's a list of Xander's achievements in Fire Emblem Fates: Conquest, alongside his small force of elite troops and royal siblings.

  • Chapter 16 (Join Chapter) - Defeated Shura's brigand ninjas aboard the ship to Hoshidan territory. Approved the decision to kill Shura for his movement-increasing Boots
  • Chapter 17 - Determined the Mokushu ninjas to be dishonorable, and thus unfit allies for Nohr. Killed their leader and daimyo, Kotaro, who had insisted on playing dirty by holding Hoshidans hostage.
  • Chapter 18 - Saved the Hoshidan royals from Zola's underhanded plot in the neutral territory of Izumo. Xander would not tolerate murdering his enemies unarmed in a neutral space, even if doing so would cripple the opposing nation. There was no honor in that. Confronted Ryoma, High Prince of Hoshido before going their separate ways.
